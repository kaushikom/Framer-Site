PRIVACY POLICY

How We Gather and Utilize Your Information 

About Us

Code Friday is a globally recognized software development firm specializing in innovative web, IoT, mobile, and AI solutions. As a data controller, we are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws, including those within the European Economic Area (EEA) where relevant. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and manage your personal data, particularly when you interact with our website (https://www.codefriday.co/) or its associated subdomains. 


This document also details your rights concerning the personal information you share with us and how to exercise them. For any questions regarding your data, reach out to us at codefridaydevacc@gmail.com. 


We value your privacy and strive to handle your information with care and integrity when you choose to share it with us. This policy provides transparency on how we use your data to enhance our services. 

What data we collect and how we use it?

We gather data that helps us recognize your device and browser, as well as track your navigation patterns and time spent on our site. This is done using the following methods: 

Cookies: These are small text files we place on your device during your visit to monitor your interactions with our site. Some cookies are temporary (session cookies), while others may persist for future visits. 

IP Address: We record your Internet Protocol address to gain insights into your general location and online behavior. 

Tracking Pixels: Embedded codes on our pages that log your visits to specific sections of the website. 

This data is used to personalize your experience and improve our content for future visits. Our legal basis for this is our legitimate interest in optimizing your interaction with our site. You can opt out of cookies via your browser settings, though this might affect some website functionalities. 

Types of Cookies We Employ

Analytics and Performance Cookies: These provide us with aggregated insights into how users navigate our site. We partner with third-party tools like Google Analytics to assess metrics such as visit frequency, referral sources, device types, and popular pages. This information helps us refine our website and is not shared with external parties for advertising, only with our service providers for analysis purposes. 

Collecting and Managing Contact Details

We may request your name and contact information (e.g., email or phone number) with your explicit consent in certain situations. This data is used exclusively to respond to your inquiries or enhance our services and is never sold or shared for marketing by third parties. Instances where you might provide this include:

  • Submitting a query through our “Get In Touch” form, where we collect your name and contact details to reply. 
  • Opting into our mailing list, where we gather your email to send newsletters or updates. 
  • Providing details for employment opportunities or other specific requests via our website forms. 

By sharing your contact information, you agree to let Code Friday use it to address your needs. This consent is our legal ground for processing. We may share this data with affiliated entities to fulfill your request, ensuring they uphold the same privacy standards. For international data transfers (e.g., outside the EEA), we implement safeguards like EU model clauses to protect your information.
 
To revoke your consent or request removal from our communications, email us at codefridaydevacc@gmail.com with your name, email, and instructions. You can also unsubscribe from newsletters by clicking the link at the bottom of our emails. 

Commonly Asked Questions

Do We Share Your Data with Other Parties? With your permission, we may share data with third-party vendors (e.g., Google Analytics) to support our services. These partners are restricted from using your data for unrelated purposes. We may also disclose information if legally required or to address issues like fraud or security breaches.
 
Is Your Data Transferred Outside the EU? If you provide personal data, it may be transferred to our affiliates or service providers globally, including outside the EEA. We ensure compliance with international data protection standards using measures like EU model clauses. 

Children and Our Website: Our site is not designed for individuals under 13. If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us at codefridaydevacc@gmail.com, and we will remove it upon confirmation. 

Data Storage and Retention

We retain your data only as long as needed for the purposes described, adhering to industry best practices to keep it secure, accurate, and relevant. This involves physical, electronic, and administrative measures to safeguard against unauthorized access or loss. 

Security Measures

Your data’s safety is paramount. We implement robust security protocols, including technical and organizational measures, to protect against breaches and ensure data integrity. 

Links to External Sites

Links to third-party websites from our site do not indicate endorsement, and we are not accountable for their content or privacy practices. This Privacy Policy applies only to our site, so please review the policies of linked sites. 

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, deletion of, or a copy of your personal data in a readable format. You may also restrict its use, request a transfer to another party, or object to marketing use. To exercise these rights, email codefridaydevacc@gmail.com. If processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it anytime, though this won’t impact prior lawful use. 

Updates to This Policy 

As laws, technologies, and our business evolve, this policy may change. We encourage periodic reviews, and the effective date will be updated to reflect revisions. 

Contact Us or File a Complaint 

For concerns or inquiries, reach us at: codefridaydevacc@gmail.com


Effective Date: April 07, 2025
